## 1. The job in one sentence

## 2. Business context (you don't need to read more than this)

Fresh For Less is a local carpet cleaning business covering Wirral, Liverpool, Birkenhead, Chester and surrounding areas. Their differentiator is **fixed-price honesty** — most competitors quote "from £X" then upsell at the door. Their offer is a flat £99 for 4 carpets with no hidden extras. They've cleaned 2,400+ carpets locally and are rated 4.9★.

The customer they're trying to reach is the household decision-maker — usually a woman aged 30–60, family home, kids and/or pets, owns the property, gets stuff done. She's seen carpet cleaning ads before; the bar to stop her scrolling is high.

## 5. Deliverables required

### Static images (3 concepts × 3 crops each)

For each of the 3 concepts below, deliver **3 aspect ratios**:
- `1:1` (1080 × 1080) — Facebook feed
- `4:5` (1080 × 1350) — Instagram feed (vertical)
- `9:16` (1080 × 1920) — Stories / Reels static

**File format:** PNG or high-quality JPG, sRGB, ≤ 30 MB each.

**File naming:** `4for99_concept-A_1x1.png`, `4for99_concept-A_4x5.png` etc.

**Critical rule:** keep text overlays **under 20% of the visible area**. Meta still demotes text-heavy ads in the auction. Use the [Facebook Text Overlay tool](https://en.facebookbrand.com/) to spot-check.

### Video (1 deliverable, 1 crop)

- **Aspect:** 9:16 (1080 × 1920)
- **Length:** 15 seconds
- **Format:** MP4, H.264, ≤ 100 MB
- **Audio:** Optional but recommended — punchy royalty-free track + natural sfx. **Burn subtitles into the video** (85% of users watch on mute).

### Concept A — Before/After split *(highest priority — start here)*

**Layout:**

- **Left side:** "BEFORE" — visibly stained section of beige/cream carpet. Slight desaturation. Small "BEFORE" label top-left in white, letter-spaced caps.
- **Right side:** "AFTER" — same carpet, deep-cleaned, rich colour. Small "AFTER" label top-right in green.

**Text overlay (top third, large bold):**
> **4 CARPETS · £99**

**Bottom-left badge** (coral pill):
> WAS £160 · SAVE 38%

**Bottom-right pill** (cream background, dark text):
> Wirral · Liverpool · Cheshire

**Logo:** Top-right, ~80px height on the 1080 frame. Don't make it the focus.

**Reference vibes:** the [Mrs Hinch cleaning before/afters](https://www.instagram.com/mrshinchhome/) — bright, satisfying, no nonsense.

---

### Concept B — Family in a clean home

**Brief:** A mum sat on a freshly-cleaned cream carpet, dog beside her, soft natural daylight. Or kid playing on the carpet, cushions, sunlight. Real-life-feeling, not stock-ad-feeling.

**Hero text overlay** (top third):
> 4 carpets cleaned for £99

**Sub-overlay** (smaller, below):
> Pet- & kid-safe · Dries in 2 hours

**Bottom strip:**
- ★ 4.9 · Fully Insured · Wirral & Cheshire
- Small `BOOK NOW →` button graphic

**If using stock:** Unsplash/Pexels search "family living room dog UK". Avoid anything that screams stock — no fake-perfect kitchens, no models in matching outfits. Real homes feel real.

**Even better:** if the client can supply a real customer photo (with permission), use that — owner-led/customer-led creative consistently outperforms stock by 2–3× on CTR.

## 7. The video — 15-second Reel

### Storyboard

| Sec | Visual | On-screen text (burnt-in) | Voiceover (optional, conversational tone) |
|---|---|---|---|
| 0–2 | Close-up: stained carpet, kid's toy/spilled crisps in frame. Slight desaturation. | **"Carpets been through it?"** (white, top) | "Carpets been through it?" |
| 2–5 | Cut to: cleaner walking in with extractor, smiles at camera. Bright, natural light. | **"We've got you."** (sliding in left) | "We've got you." |
| 5–9 | Time-lapse: the dirty patch from sec 0–2 → clean. ~2-3 second satisfying transformation. | **"4 carpets · £99"** (large, fresh-green) | "Four carpets professionally deep-cleaned for ninety-nine quid." |
| 9–12 | Wide shot: mum + dog walking on dry, fresh carpet. Warm tones. | **"Dries in 2 hrs"** + small **"Hot-water extraction"** | "Hot-water extraction. Dries in two hours. Stains, smells, gone." |
| 12–15 | End card: brand-blue background, logo, big £99, phone `0330 043 4811`, URL, **BOOK NOW →** button. | **"4for99 · Only 7 slots this week"** + URL + phone | "Tap below — only seven slots left this week." |

### Style notes
- Cuts every 2–3 seconds, no longer. Reels punish slow openings.
- First 3 seconds *must* hook — show the dirty carpet immediately, not the logo.
- Subtitles burnt in, big enough to read at thumb-width. Bottom 25% of frame is danger zone (covered by IG UI) — keep text out of there.
- End card on screen for the full last 3 seconds (not just 1) — Meta needs to register the CTA.

## 9. What success looks like

After delivery, this creative will run as paid Facebook/Instagram ads to a cold local audience.

**Designer's job is "done well" if:**
- The creative achieves **CTR ≥ 1.5%** (link clicks ÷ impressions) within the first 1,000 impressions
- The before/after concept ideally outperforms the price-card concept (this is the industry pattern for cleaning services)

**Designer's job is "done badly" if:**
- Price is unreadable on a phone screen at thumbnail size
- Logo competes with the offer for attention
- More than 20% of frame area is text
